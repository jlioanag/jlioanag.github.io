# Ereaders

## Wants

1. Read manga and books
2. Sufficient storage for manga
3. Servicable battery life
4. Customization

### Not necessary

1. Audiobook support
2. Video playback
3. Internet browsing

## Notes

Most pocket readers will probably not fit the manga criteria since they are phone sized with unoptimized zoom support (especially for eink).
