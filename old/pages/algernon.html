Exploring music production and theory

What is this?

Since 2017, I have been tinkering with FL Studio, a digital audio workstation (DAW), to create short concepts in order to further develop my knowledge of production and practice theory. I have been learning theory through free online means such as YouTube videos, but I hope to supplement these with more music courses at RPI. Below you can find some of my creations.

Jeamu (Te Amo)

julian algernon · Jeamu (Te Amo)

Inspired by Madlib and MF DOOM production, I wanted to try making some more sampled hip-hop beats. This track uses simple looped sample excerpts with added drums for extra punch. Also added a beatswitch just for fun. The intro is a reversed version of the opening sample loop with pitch automation to mimic tempo change.

Jeamu X GAZILLION EAR (MF DOOM)

julian algernon · Jeamu X GAZILLION EAR

Why not? Suprised with how well it fits in my opinion. I would fix DOOM's vocals to fit the timing of the second section better and remove the sample vocals.

Chanel (Frank Ocean) Remix

julian algernon · Chanel (Julian's Remix)

My first delve into dance. Inspired by "American Boy" mashups with "Chanel", I wanted to try my hand at remixing. I want to fix the levels since the bass synth is hard to hear and the snare is way too loud. I also want to add more variation in the composition since the same 4-on-the-floor beat plays throughout.

Gaia (Demo)

julian algernon · Gaia(Demo)

An experimental hip-hop track inspired by Death Grips' The Money Store. I flipped a bossa nova song using granular synthesis to create a glitchy, ominous feel. My main concern with this track is that the drums and bass synth don't stand out compared to the distorted samples. The intro is also dragged out quite long.

Float

julian algernon · float

The first piece I uploaded to SoundCloud. It's a generic lo-fi hip-hop beat but it serves its purpose as study music in my opinion. The main thing I learned from making "float" was proper chord progression and structure. If I could find the project file for this song and fix it, I would improve the mix, as the hi-hat stands out too much. Also, I think I accidentally side-chained it haha.
